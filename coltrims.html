Research and learning area

COLTRIMS & Data Acquisition

This section examines the COLTRIMS experimental method and the data acquisition systems used to measure charged-particle momenta in coincidence. It connects spectrometer fields, time-of-flight measurements, position-sensitive detectors, timing electronics, coincidence detection, calibration, and event acquisition with the reconstruction of molecular fragmentation processes.
