Research toolkit

Imaging molecular dynamics through momentum.

My research combines ultrafast light, coincidence detection, and momentum-resolved imaging to investigate molecular fragmentation.

01

Coulomb Explosion Imaging

Mapping molecular fragmentation through the momentum of charged fragments produced during rapid ionization.

02

Femtosecond Lasers

Using ultrashort laser pulses to initiate and probe dynamics on the natural timescales of molecular motion.

03

COLTRIMS

Measuring coincident charged-particle momenta with a COLTRIMS reaction microscope.
