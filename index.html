Graduate Research Assistant Atomic, Molecular and Optical Physics James R. Macdonald Laboratory, Department of Physics, Kansas State University

I am currently a Graduate Research Assistant at the James R. Macdonald Laboratory in the Department of Physics at Kansas State University. My research focuses on Atomic, Molecular, and Optical Physics, with a specialization in ultrafast femtosecond laser science. I study changes in molecular structure and dynamics induced by interactions with ultrafast femtosecond laser pulses.
