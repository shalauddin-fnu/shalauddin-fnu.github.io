  <h2 id="selected-projects">Selected projects</h2>

  <div class="projects-list">
    <article class="project-card project-cyan">
      <span class="project-number">Project 01</span>

      <h2>Ultrafast Dynamics of Iodomethane</h2>

      <p>
        This project investigates excitation, ionization, and dissociation
        in iodomethane (CH<sub>3</sub>I) using two-color femtosecond
        pump-probe spectroscopy. Coincident fragment measurements are used
        to distinguish intensity-dependent dynamics, including vibrational
        excitation, predissociation, and dissociative-ionization pathways.
      </p>

      <p class="project-role">PhD research project</p>
    </article>

    <article class="project-card project-violet">
      <span class="project-number">Project 02</span>

      <h2>Ultraviolet Photodynamics of 1,2-Dichloroethylene</h2>

      <p>
        This collaborative project examines the photoinduced dynamics of
        the cis and trans isomers of 1,2-dichloroethylene using
        time-resolved Coulomb explosion imaging. Fragment momenta, ion
        yields, and kinetic-energy distributions are used to investigate
        isomerization and competing molecular dissociation pathways.
      </p>

      <p class="project-role">Collaborative research project</p>
    </article>

    <article class="project-card project-gold">
      <span class="project-number">Project 03</span>

      <h2>Coherent Vibrational Dynamics in Trifluoroiodomethane</h2>

      <p>
        This collaborative project investigates coherent vibrational
        motion in trifluoroiodomethane (CF<sub>3</sub>I) using
        time-resolved Coulomb explosion imaging. Time-dependent
        fragment-momentum correlations provide structural information
        about laser-induced nuclear dynamics.
      </p>

      <p class="project-role">Collaborative research project</p>
    </article>
