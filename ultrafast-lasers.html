Ultrafast Lasers & Experimental Methods

This section explores the principles and experimental methods of ultrafast laser science, including femtosecond pulse generation and propagation, dispersion, nonlinear frequency conversion, pump-probe timing, and optical alignment. These concepts are connected to experimental techniques for investigating molecular dynamics on ultrafast timescales.
